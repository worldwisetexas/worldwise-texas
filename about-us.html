About Us

WorldWise Texas helps students and adults learn about countries, cultures, and geography through short videos and simple explanations.

Our Mission

WorldWise Texas is an educational project created to help people learn about countries around the world, their cultures, and their geography in a clear and engaging way.

We believe that learning about the world should be simple, interesting, and useful for students, families, and anyone who wants to understand more about the places and people that shape our planet.

Meet the Founder

WorldWise Texas was founded by Rohan Balan, a student at Seven Lakes High School in the Class of 2029.

After hearing his peers express confusion about geography and cultures worldwide, he made it his mission to educate students and adults about countries across the world and the cultures that make each place unique.

Through WorldWise Texas, Rohan hopes to make geography more accessible, more interesting, and more meaningful for everyone.

Why Learn Geography?

Geography helps citizens be more educated about our world. Learning and appreciating others contributes heavily to our public face and promotes interconnection and understanding between people.

Geography also helps us understand how countries, cultures, and environments are connected. When we learn more about the world, we become more thoughtful, more informed, and more aware of the people and places beyond our own community.

That kind of knowledge matters because it helps build respect, curiosity, and a stronger understanding of the world we share.
