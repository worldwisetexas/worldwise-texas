About Us

WorldWise Texas is an educational project created to help students and adults learn about countries around the world, their cultures, and their geography through short videos and simple explanations.

We believe that learning about the world should be easy to understand, interesting, and useful for everyday life.

Meet the Founder

WorldWise Texas was founded by Rohan Balan, a student at Seven Lakes High School, Class of 2029.

After hearing his peers be confused about geography and cultures worldwide, he made it his mission to educate students and adults about countries across the world and their cultures.

Why Learn Geography?

Geography helps citizens be more educated about our world. Learning and appreciating others contributes heavily to our public face and promotes interconnection and understanding between people.

Geography also helps us understand how countries, cultures, and environments connect. It builds curiosity, respect, and awareness of the world beyond our own community.
